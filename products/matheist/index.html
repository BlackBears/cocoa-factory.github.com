mAtheist

Jan 10th, 2013

mAtheist for iOS is a resource for freethinkers looking for a better understand of their world view. We released the app for iPhone back in 2009; and through several versions, it’s in the hands of thousands of people world wide.

Features

mAtheist a number of features:

  • Quotes - the app contains over 900 inspirational and informative quotes from some of history’s best-known and insightful freethinkers. We add new quotes weekly; and the app automatically fetches them from our database.
    • Share quotes - you can share quotes by email, Facebook, Twitter, and Messages.
    • Favorites - mark your favorite quotes for easy reference. You can also find quotes that other users like.
    • Search - search for just the right quote for the occasion.
  • Contradictions - a list of contradictions found in the Bible. The contradictions are fully-referenced; and you can read the verses that pertain to the contradiction.
  • Absurdities and oddities - a growing list of unusual happenings in the Bible.
  • Logical fallacies - a quick reference of fallacies commonly used both in apologetics and daily discourse.
